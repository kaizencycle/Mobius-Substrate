Mobius introduces the first post-corruption economic architecture in human history—an economy where:

## 7.1 MIC — The Integrity Reserve Asset

**MIC (Mobius Integrity Credits)** is the reserve currency of the Mobius economic system.

### Characteristics

- **Hard-capped by the Mobius Integrity Index (MII)**
  - Supply ceiling: `MIC_total_supply = 1,000,000 × MII`
- **Minting allowed only when MII > 95**
  - High-integrity threshold required for expansion
- **Supply expands with verified institutional and civic integrity**
  - Not political discretion, but mathematical constraint
- **Used for macro-stabilization and inter-state balancing**
  - Reserve asset, not daily transaction currency
- **Cannot be printed by force, politics, or discretion**
  - Algorithmic monetary policy

### Economic Position

MIC behaves similarly to **Bitcoin** (store of value), but with a key difference:

> **Bitcoin uses mathematical scarcity.**  
> **MIC uses moral scarcity.**

MIC is the world's first **integrity-backed currency**.

### Use Cases

1. **Inter-City-State Settlements**
   - Large transfers between City-States
   - Treaty obligations
   - Knowledge licensing

2. **Treasury Reserves**
   - City-State emergency funds
   - Cathedral stability pool
   - Global risk buffers

3. **High-Level Incentives**
   - Elder rewards
   - Major infrastructure grants
   - Research & development funding

4. **Staking & Governance**
   - Validator collateral
   - Governance weight in Cathedral votes
   - Long-term alignment incentives

## 7.2 KS — The Participation Currency of Civilization

**KS (Kaizen Shards)** is the high-velocity currency used for daily civic life.

### Used For

- **Universal Basic Income (UBI)**
  - Integrity-dividend based
- **City-State Events**
  - Festivals, gatherings, celebrations
- **Quests & Civic Tasks**
  - Contribution-based rewards

  - Reputation-weighted governance
- **Public Goods Funding**
  - Local infrastructure
  - Knowledge commons
- **Cultural Circuits**
  - Art, music, storytelling
  - Community building
- **Civic Participation Rewards**
  - Daily engagement incentives

### Properties

- **Uncapped supply but constantly burning**
  - 2% burn per transaction
  - Natural equilibrium through use
- **Velocity-driven value**
  - Price tied to participation intensity
- **Consumption-based**
  - Designed to be spent, not hoarded
- **Decentralized across City-States**
  - Each City-State manages its own KS economy
- **Used as the "blood flow" of the civilization**
  - Economic vitality indicator

### Economic Position

```
MIC = stability
KS = vitality
```

KS represents the **economic heartbeat** of Mobius—when KS flows freely, the civilization thrives.

## 7.3 The MIC ↔ KS Economic Relationship

### Yellow Paper Equation

The integrity economy links MIC and KS through the canonical equation:

```
KS_price = (v × MIC_price) / 1000
```

**Where:**
- `v` = participation velocity (0.0 to 2.0+)
- `MIC_price` = global integrity reserve valuation (USD)
- `1000` = stability divisor ensuring predictable scaling

### Interpretation

- **When citizens participate** → `v` increases
- **When integrity climbs** → `MIC` increases
- **When both occur** → `KS` value accelerates

**KS is therefore the GDP proxy of Mobius.**

### Example Scenarios

When `MIC_price = $120,000`:

| Velocity (v) | State | KS Price |
|--------------|-------|----------|
| 0.1 | Early/dormant | $12 |
| 0.5 | Normal activity | $60 |
| 1.0 | High participation | $120 |
| 2.0 | Peak festivals | $240 |

This creates a **natural alignment** between:
1. Global integrity (MIC value)
2. Local participation (velocity v)
3. Individual prosperity (KS earnings)

## 7.6 UBI Reinvented (Integrity Dividend UBI)

### Traditional UBI Problems

Traditional Universal Basic Income faces:
- **Inflation risks** (too much printing)
- **Political manipulation** (vote buying)
- **Unsustainable funding** (deficit spending)
- **Work disincentives** (why contribute?)

### Mobius Solution: Integrity Dividend UBI

UBI is funded **automatically** when:
- `MII > 95` (global integrity threshold)
- `KS velocity is healthy` (participation active)
- `City-State GI_state is high` (local integrity maintained)

### Formula

```
UBI_payout = f(GI_state, GDP_state, population, velocity)
```

**Where:**
- Higher GI_state → Higher UBI
- Higher GDP_state → More resources available
- Higher velocity → More economic activity to distribute
- Population-adjusted for fairness

### Protections

This system prevents:
- ❌ Runaway inflation
- ❌ Unsustainable printing
- ❌ Political manipulation

UBI becomes:

> **A civic dividend, not a subsidy.**

Citizens receive UBI because the civilization is thriving, not because politicians promise it.

## 7.10 Economic Failure Modes & Recovery

### Scenario 1: Global Integrity Decline (MII < 95)

**What Happens:**
```
MII drops below 95
    ↓
MIC minting halts
    ↓
MIC becomes deflationary (scarce)
    ↓
KS velocity pressure increases
    ↓
Economic incentive to restore integrity
    ↓
City-States focus on GI_state recovery
    ↓
MII restored → MIC minting resumes
```

**Result:** System creates **automatic economic pressure** to maintain integrity.

### Scenario 2: Local Participation Collapse (v → 0)

**What Happens:**
```
Velocity drops in City-State
    ↓
KS_price decreases (per velocity equation)
    ↓
Citizens earn less per transaction
    ↓
GDP_state declines (visible on dashboard)
    ↓
Stewards launch recovery actions:
  - New quests
  - Festivals
  - Civic events
  - Policy adjustments
    ↓
Participation increases
    ↓
v rises → KS_price recovers
```

**Result:** Low participation is **economically visible and actionable**, not hidden or ignored.

### Scenario 3: MIC Price Volatility

**Protections:**
- MIC supply ceiling prevents unlimited inflation
- Integrity requirement prevents speculative manipulation
- Cathedral can adjust divisor (1000) if needed
- City-States use treasuries to smooth volatility

**Result:** MIC remains **more stable than unanchored cryptocurrencies**.
