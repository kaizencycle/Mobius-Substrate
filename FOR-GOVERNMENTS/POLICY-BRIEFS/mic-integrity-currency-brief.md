## Executive Summary

The **Mobius Integrity Credit (MIC)** represents a novel approach to monetary policy: a currency backed not by gold, oil, or government decree, but by verifiable integrity actions. This brief outlines how MIC can serve as a complementary monetary tool for:

1. **National Debt Reduction** through order creation rather than borrowing
2. **Civic Engagement Incentives** aligned with social stability
3. **AI Safety Infrastructure** that benefits all stakeholders

## The Solution: MIC Currency

### What is MIC?

**Mobius Integrity Credits (MIC)** are digital currency units minted when actions demonstrably increase system integrity.

**Core Formula:**
```
MIC_minted = α × max(0, MII - τ) × ActionValue
```

Where:
- **α** = Base coefficient (1.0)
- **MII** = Mobius Integrity Index (0–1 scale)
- **τ** = Threshold (0.95)
- **ActionValue** = Weighted contribution score

### Key Properties

| Property | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Non-Inflationary** | Only minted when integrity increases |
| **Verifiable** | All minting requires cryptographic proof |
| **Scalable** | Works at individual, corporate, and national levels |
| **Complementary** | Operates alongside existing currencies |

## Implementation Roadmap

### Phase 1: Pilot Program (12 months)
- **Scale:** 50,000 participants
- **Scope:** One federal agency + three municipalities
- **Metrics:** Participation rates, MII scores, satisfaction
- **Budget:** $5M operational costs

### Phase 2: Expansion (24 months)
- **Scale:** 500,000 participants
- **Scope:** Multiple agencies, cross-state
- **Integration:** Tax system, benefits disbursement
- **Budget:** $25M with projected $50M savings

### Phase 3: National Deployment (36+ months)
- **Scale:** Full citizen access
- **Scope:** Federal, state, local coordination
- **Integration:** Digital ID, banking systems
- **Budget:** Revenue-neutral through efficiency gains

## Risk Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| **Gaming** | Multi-sentinel verification, caps per cycle |
| **Privacy** | Local-first architecture, hash-only sharing |
| **Complexity** | Phased rollout, extensive training |
| **Resistance** | Voluntary participation, clear benefits |

## Recommendation

We recommend Congress authorize a **12-month federal pilot program** with:

1. **$5M appropriation** for infrastructure and operations
2. **50,000 participant cap** across selected agencies
3. **Independent evaluation** by GAO or equivalent
4. **Quarterly reports** to oversight committees
5. **Clear success metrics** defined in advance

### Expected Outcomes

- Proof-of-concept for integrity-backed currency
- Data on citizen engagement effectiveness
- Framework for broader deployment
- International leadership in AI governance
