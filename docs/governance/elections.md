# Elections

Annual STV for Council seats; biannual for TSC where needed. Eligibility and voter rolls defined in Bylaws. COI statements required prior to seating.

## Voter Eligibility

- ≥3 merged PRs in past 12 months, OR
- Recognized adopter organization representative, OR
- Active working group participant

## Candidate Requirements

- Contributor for ≥6 months
- Signed CLA
- No unresolved COI
- Statement of intent and platform

## Process

1. Nomination period (14 days)
2. Candidate Q&A (7 days)
3. Voting period (7 days, anonymous STV)
4. Results announced
5. Transition period (7 days)

## Schedule

- Council: Annual in January
- TSC: As needed (typically July)
