- **Clear standards** ‚Äî every tier has objective requirements

- **Dynamic hierarchy** ‚Äî City-States can rise or fall

- **AGI integration** ‚Äî AI grows alongside human civilization

- **Arbitrary hierarchy** ‚Äî all measurement is transparent
- **Hopelessness** ‚Äî every tier has a path upward

## üúå XII. RELEGATION AND DESCENT

**The ladder works both ways.**

### Tier Demotion Triggers:

**Automatic Demotion:**
- GI_state drops 2+ points below tier minimum for 30+ days
- Multiple Lotus Cycles within 6 months
- Catastrophic integrity violations
- Economic collapse (GDP drops 50%+)
- Mass citizen exodus (>30% in 30 days)

**Relegation Process:**
1. Warning issued by Cathedral
2. 60-day grace period for correction
3. Emergency support provided
4. If uncorrected ‚Üí demotion by 1 tier
5. Lotus Cycle initiated
6. Recovery pathway established

**Note:** Demotion is not punishment ‚Äî it is recalibration.
