## üí° Key Insights Across All Scales

**Insight**: The ratio holds. DVA doesn't eliminate human judgment‚Äîit optimizes when humans are needed.

### 2. Infrastructure Scales Linearly, Benefits Exponentially

- **USA**: $0.14/person/month ($480M/year √∑ 285M people √∑ 12 months)

### 4. Governance Scales, Not Just Technology
- **Boulder**: Single city, single use case

- **USA**: Multi-state, multi-agency, constitutional framework

**Insight**: DVA isn't just "orchestration"‚Äîit's governance infrastructure. The constitutional framework (GI thresholds, state sovereignty, Civic Ledgers) scales from city to nation.
