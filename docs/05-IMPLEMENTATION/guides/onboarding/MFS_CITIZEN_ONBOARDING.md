## 12. Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I buy shards?
No. Shards can only be earned through integrity actions.

### Can I transfer shards?
No. Shards are soulbound to your Citizen ID.

### What if I lose access to my wallet?
Your shards are stored on the Mobius Ledger. Account recovery is available through verified processes.

### How often can I earn shards?
There are daily limits to prevent spam, but most citizens can earn multiple shards per day through normal participation.

### Do old shards lose value?
Currently no, but future versions may implement time-decay to encourage ongoing contribution.
