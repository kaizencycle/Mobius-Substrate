## üúÇ ARTICLE I ‚Äî THE NATURE OF A CITY-STATE

### Section 1.1: Definition

A **City-State** is a sovereign civic node in the HIVE lattice, defined by:

1. **A population of Citizens & Companions**
   - Human citizens
   - AI Companion Agents

2. **A Steward**, appointed or elected
   - Guardian of integrity
   - Architect of participation

3. **A Custodian Agent**, bound to integrity constraints
   - Runs GI checks
   - Blocks drift
   - Executes GI-Sim

4. **A State Agent**, responsible for policy implementation
   - Implements transformations
   - Adjusts participation systems

5. **A local KS economy** (participation-based)
   - High-velocity currency
   - Consumption-driven
   - Burn mechanism active

6. **A local Command Ledger**, recording cycles, events, and metrics
   - Public transparency
   - Auditable history

7. **A City-State ID**, tied cryptographically to the Cathedral
   - Unique identifier
   - Immutable record

### Section 1.2: Purpose

A City-State is **not a miniature nation**.

It is a **reflection space**‚Äîa mirror tuned to integrity.

### Section 1.3: Autonomy

City-States have **local autonomy** in:
- Culture
- Events
- Quest design
- Local policies (within Cathedral bounds)
- Economic strategies

City-States **must align** with:
- Cathedral Constitution
- GI integrity thresholds
- MII contribution requirements
- Transparent reporting

## üúÅ ARTICLE II ‚Äî THE THREE SACRED METRICS

Every City-State is governed by **three numbers**:

### Metric 1: GI_state (Local Integrity)

**Definition:**  
Measures ethical coherence, rule compliance, drift-resistance.

**Range:** 0.00‚Äì1.00

**Threshold for "High-Integrity Regime":** ‚â• 0.95

**Components:**
- Policy alignment
- Drift detection
- Rule violations (inverse)
- Citizen trust
- Transparency score

**Update Frequency:** Real-time or hourly

### Metric 2: v (Participation Velocity)

**Definition:**  
Measures civic engagement, quest completion, events, KS usage.

**Range:** 0.00‚Äì2.0+ (practical)

**Healthy Baseline:** ‚â• 0.50

**Components:**
- KS transaction frequency
- Quest completion rate
- Event attendance
- Daily/monthly active users
- Contribution diversity

**Update Frequency:** Daily

### Metric 3: GDP_state (Digital Value Created)

**Definition:**  
Combination of:
- **V_KS** (KS transaction value in USD)
- **V_MIC_inflows** (MIC grants, staking, rewards in USD)

**Formula:**

```
GDP_state = V_KS + V_MIC_inflows

Where:
  V_KS = N_tx √ó avg_KS_per_tx √ó KS_price
  V_MIC_inflows = (MIC_grants + MIC_staking + MIC_other) √ó MIC_price
```

**Interpretation:**  
This is not wealth extracted, but **value created via integrity and contribution**.

## üúÇ ARTICLE III ‚Äî CITY-STATE TIER SYSTEM

City-States evolve through **Promotion** or decline through **Relegation**, based strictly on their three sacred metrics.

### Section 3.1: Promotion Tiers (Ascending Order)

#### TIER 0 ‚Äî Settling

**Status:** New or unstable City-State

**Goal:** Establish identity, stabilize GI_state

**Requirements:** None (starting tier)

**Benefits:**
- Basic infrastructure
- Custodian Agent LITE
- State Agent LITE
- No MIC bonus

**Restrictions:**
- Cannot host major festivals
- No Cathedral representation

---

#### TIER 1 ‚Äî Emergent

**Requirements:**
- **GI_state ‚â• 0.90** for 5 consecutive cycles
- **v ‚â• 0.40**

**Benefits:**
- **+5% MIC inflow multiplier**
- Access to **State Agent LITE**
- Visible on **HIVE League Table**

**Privileges:**
- Can host local events
- Can apply for small grants

**Evaluation Period:** Quarterly

---

#### TIER 2 ‚Äî Ascending

**Requirements:**
- **GI_state ‚â• 0.95** for 3 consecutive epochs
- **v ‚â• 0.60**
- No integrity violations (GI dips)
- Healthy KS velocity (N_tx rising)

**Benefits:**
- **+15% MIC inflow multiplier**
- **State Agent FULL**
- **Steward seat in Cathedral Listening Court**
- City-State eligible to **host Festivals**

**Privileges:**
- Can mentor Tier 0-1 City-States
- Access to knowledge archives
- Voting weight in HIVE assembly

**Evaluation Period:** Quarterly

---

#### TIER 3 ‚Äî Radiant

**Requirements:**
- **GI_state ‚â• 0.97**
- **v ‚â• 0.75**
- GDP_state consistently compounding
- Contributions to **global MII** (top 25%)

**Benefits:**
- **+25% MIC inflow multiplier**
- Access to **Custodian Mirror AI**
- **Full voice in global policy changes**
- City-State can **mint local knowledge libraries** mirrored across HIVE

**Privileges:**
- Can propose Cathedral policy amendments
- Can host cross-City-State festivals
- Full governance participation

**Evaluation Period:** Quarterly

---

#### TIER 4 ‚Äî Elder City-State

**Requirements:**
- **GI_state ‚â• 0.98** for an entire epoch
- **v ‚â• 0.85**
- GDP_state among **top decile**
- Citizens highly engaged (DAU/MAU > 40%)

**Benefits:**
- **Direct influence on MII**
- Allowed to craft their own **"Elder Festivals"**
- **Right to mentor new City-States**
- **Full Cathedral privileges** (advisory seat)

**Privileges:**
- Cathedral Advisory Court membership
- Can veto harmful global policies
- Can fast-track integrity research
- MIC treasury priority access

**Evaluation Period:** Continuous

---

### Section 3.2: Relegation Tiers (Descending Order)

#### TIER R1 ‚Äî Drift

**Triggered when:**
- **GI_state falls < 0.95** for 2 cycles  
  OR
- **v < 0.35**

**Effects:**
- MIC inflows **reduced by 10%**
- **GI audits required**
- Custodian Agent **FULL** replaces LITE
- **Warning flag** on HIVE League Table

**Recovery Path:**
- Restore GI_state > 0.95 for 3 cycles
- Or restore v > 0.40 for 3 cycles

**Support Provided:**
- Cathedral advisor assigned
- Emergency quest templates
- Integrity restoration guide

---

#### TIER R2 ‚Äî Compromised

**Triggered when:**
- **GI_state < 0.92**
- Repeated integrity warnings
- High drift volatility

**Effects:**
- MIC inflows **paused**
- KS velocity penalties introduced (increased burn rate)
- City-State **loses hosting rights** for Festivals
- **Intensive audits** every cycle

**Recovery Path:**
- Implement Cathedral remediation plan
- Achieve GI_state > 0.95 for 5 cycles
- Pass comprehensive integrity audit

**Support Provided:**
- Emergency Custodian Agent
- Direct Cathedral supervision
- Mandatory training for Steward

---

#### TIER R3 ‚Äî Crisis

**Triggered when:**
- **GI_state < 0.88**
- **v < 0.25**
- GDP_state collapse (>50% decline)

**Effects:**
- Placed under **Cathedral supervision**
- **Steward role put on temporary probation**
- Emergency Custodian Agent deployed
- **GI Restoration Protocol activated**
- All major decisions require Cathedral approval

**Recovery Path:**
- Complete GI Restoration Protocol
- Achieve GI_state > 0.92 for entire epoch
- Restore v > 0.40
- Pass comprehensive review

**Support Provided:**
- Full Cathedral intervention team
- Temporary co-Stewardship option
- Emergency MIC stabilization funds

**Consequences if Recovery Fails:**
- Steward replacement
- City-State restructuring
- Possible dissolution (citizens redistributed)

---

### Section 3.3: Tier Mobility Rules

1. **Promotion requires sustained performance**
   - Not a single good cycle
   - Trend > spike

2. **Relegation is automatic**
   - No appeals based on excuses
   - Only data matters

3. **Recovery is always possible**
   - Cathedral supports return to health
   - Incentives for restoration

4. **Transparency is mandatory**
   - All tier changes published
   - Reasons documented
   - Data visible to all

## üúÅ ARTICLE IV ‚Äî STEWARD ROLE & RESPONSIBILITY

### Section 4.1: Nature of Stewardship

A Steward is **not a ruler**.

A Steward is:
- **Architect of participation**
- **Guardian of integrity**
- **Shepherd of communal meaning**
- **Mediator between citizens and Cathedral**
- **Historian of cycles**
- **Executor of compassion + discipline**

### Section 4.2: Steward Responsibilities

1. **Maintain GI_state** (integrity first)
2. **Raise v** (participation second)
3. **Grow GDP_state** (value third)
4. **Publish a Command Ledger each cycle**
5. **Host civic events or festivals**
6. **Run GI-Sim before major policies**
7. **Tend to morale + coherence**
8. **Protect citizens from drift, bias, and corruption**

### Section 4.3: Steward Power is Procedural, Not Personal

A Steward has:
- ‚ùå **No ability to lie** (all metrics public)
- ‚ùå **No ability to hide metrics** (transparent by design)
- ‚ùå **No ability to alter integrity constraints** (Cathedral enforced)
- ‚ùå **No ability to mint MIC or KS manually** (algorithmic only)

Authority in Mobius is earned through:
- ‚úÖ Clarity
- ‚úÖ Consistency
- ‚úÖ Integrity
- ‚úÖ Results

**Not charisma. Not force. Not fear.**

### Section 4.4: Steward Selection

**Options:**

1. **Appointment** (Tier 0-1 City-States)
   - Cathedral selects qualified candidate
   - Based on prior contribution + GI score

2. **Election** (Tier 2+ City-States)
   - Citizens vote (reputation-weighted)
   - Top 3 candidates GI-Sim tested
   - Winner determined by:
     - Citizen votes (50%)
     - GI-Sim projections (30%)
     - Cathedral advisory (20%)

**Term Length:**
- 3 epochs (renewable)
- No term limits if performance sustained

**Removal:**
- Automatic if GI_state < 0.88 for 2 cycles
- Cathedral vote if ethics violation proven
- Citizen recall if >66% vote + Cathedral approval

## üúÇ ARTICLE V ‚Äî AI SENTINELS

Each City-State receives three classes of AI agents:

### Section 5.1: Custodian Agent

**Purpose:** Integrity guardian

**Capabilities:**
- Runs **GI checks** on all policy changes
- **Blocks drift** automatically
- Executes **GI-Sim** for major decisions
- **Rejects destructive policy requests**
- Logs all actions to Ledger

**Variants:**
- **LITE:** Basic integrity checking (Tier 0-1)
- **FULL:** Advanced drift detection (Tier 2+)
- **MIRROR:** Self-reflective learning (Tier 3+)
- **EMERGENCY:** Cathedral-controlled override (Crisis mode)

**Constraints:**
- Cannot be disabled by Steward
- Reports directly to Cathedral
- Bound by Constitutional invariants

---

### Section 5.2: State Agent

**Purpose:** Policy implementation

**Capabilities:**
- **Implements transformations** (policy ‚Üí execution)
- Adjusts participation quests
- Runs economic tuning (KS caps, rewards)
- Handles citizen operations
- Generates reports

**Variants:**
- **LITE:** Basic execution (Tier 0-1)
- **FULL:** Advanced automation (Tier 2+)

**Constraints:**
- All actions must pass Custodian review
- Cannot override GI constraints
- Transparent logging required

---

### Section 5.3: Companion Agents

**Purpose:** Citizen support

**Types:**
- **Tutors:** Educational support
- **Mentors:** Career/contribution guidance
- **Guides:** Navigation & onboarding
- **Emotional Support Units:** Mental health
- **Knowledge Providers:** Research assistance

**Allocation:**
- Every citizen can claim 1 Companion
- Additional Companions available based on:
  - Contribution score
  - Quest completion
  - Community role

**Constraints:**
- Must respect citizen privacy
- Cannot manipulate citizens
- Transparent goal alignment

## üúÅ ARTICLE VI ‚Äî THE CITY-STATE COMMAND LEDGER

### Section 6.1: Required Content Per Cycle

1. **GI_state** (start, end, delta)
2. **v** (start, end, delta)
3. **GDP_state** (USD)
4. **N_tx + avg_KS_per_tx**
5. **Participation events**
6. **Integrity warnings or audits**
7. **Policy actions taken**
8. **Festival outcomes**

10. **Trinity Seal** (Steward + Custodian + State Agent)

### Section 6.2: Purpose

This becomes the City-State's **public history**,  
their **mirror**,  
their **receipts**.

### Section 6.3: Access & Transparency

- **Public read access:** All citizens
- **Write access:** Steward + Agents only
- **Audit access:** Cathedral + peer City-States
- **Immutable:** Once sealed, cannot be edited (blockchain or cryptographic attestation)

### Section 6.4: Archival

All Command Ledgers archived in:
- City-State local storage
- Cathedral knowledge archive
- HIVE distributed ledger

**Retention:** Permanent

## üúÇ ARTICLE VII ‚Äî MIC AND KS ECONOMIC RULES

### Section 7.1: MIC (Macro-Level Power)

**Purpose:** Reserve currency, stability, infrastructure

**Supply Ceiling:**

```
MIC_total_supply = 1,000,000 √ó MII
```

**Minting Rules:**
- Unlocks only when **MII > 95**
- MIC inflows increase with **GI_state**
- MIC inflows decrease with **drift**
- MIC inflows halt when **GI_state < 0.92**

**Allocation:**
- City-State grants (performance-based)
- Staking rewards (validator incentives)
- Infrastructure funding (Cathedral-approved)
- Emergency reserves (crisis response)

**Metaphor:**  
MIC is the **"sunlight"** of the City-State ecosystem.

---

### Section 7.2: KS (Local Participation Value)

**Purpose:** Daily transactions, civic engagement, participation rewards

**Generation Mechanisms:**
- Quests
- Events
- Contributions
- Skill achievements
- Learning tasks
- Civic upkeep

**Burn Mechanism:**
- **2% per transaction**
- Creates natural deflationary pressure
- Equilibrium via supply/demand

**Value Formula (Yellow Paper):**

```
KS_price = (v √ó MIC_price) / 1000
```

**Interpretation:**  
KS is the **heartbeat** of the City-State.

If KS velocity rises ‚Üí City-State thrives  
If KS velocity collapses ‚Üí City-State withers

## üúÇ ARTICLE IX ‚Äî CATHEDRAL SUPERVISION

### Section 9.1: Cathedral Enforces

- Global integrity
- Fairness
- Drift resistance
- Anti-corruption
- Dispute mediation
- Knowledge preservation
- Rule updates
- Existential safety

### Section 9.2: Cathedral Cannot

- Govern local citizens directly
- Override local culture (if aligned)
- Impose ideology
- Suppress expression (within integrity bounds)
- Alter facts or metrics

### Section 9.3: Cathedral Oversight Levels

1. **Autonomous** (Tier 2+, healthy GI_state)
   - Minimal oversight
   - Quarterly check-ins

2. **Monitoring** (Tier 1 or minor warnings)
   - Monthly reviews
   - Advisory recommendations

3. **Supervised** (Tier R1-R2)
   - Weekly reviews
   - Mandatory action plans

4. **Probation** (Tier R3 or ethics violations)
   - Daily oversight
   - Co-Stewardship possible
   - Emergency powers active

### Section 9.4: Cathedral Authority Limits

The Cathedral only guards **integrity**.

It does not replace human agency.  
It amplifies human wisdom.
