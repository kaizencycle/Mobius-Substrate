## Key Concepts

### Time Security Index (TSI)
Can people predict their financial situation 6+ months ahead?

### Crisis Amplitude Reduction (CAR)
How violent are economic downturns?

### Panic Half-Life (PHL)
How long does economic fear persist after shocks?

### Family Stability Coefficient (FSC)
Are families able to remain connected?

### Creative Output Multiplier (COM)
How much creative work emerges when survival pressure drops?

### Integrity Velocity Index (IVI)
How quickly does trust recover after disruption?
