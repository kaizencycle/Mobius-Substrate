## Available Drafts

### Bank of England (Negentropy) Act

**Status**: Draft ready for HM Treasury review

**Purpose**: Authorize the Bank of England to integrate negentropy-based debt management.

**Key Provisions**:

1. **Part 1: Definitions**
   - "Negentropy" means verified order creation as measured by the Mobius Integrity Index
   - "Integrity Gateway" means the technical connection between BoE systems and Mobius infrastructure

2. **Part 2: Powers**
   - BoE may establish pilot programs for negentropy integration
   - BoE may recognize MIC as settlement utility (not legal tender)
   - BoE may publish negentropy accounting standards

3. **Part 3: Oversight**
   - FCA oversight of sentinel network operations
   - Annual reporting to Parliament
   - Independent audit requirements

4. **Part 4: Safeguards**
   - Human override preserved
   - Exit mechanisms for participants
   - Privacy protections

**Committee**: HM Treasury

### AI Safety (Integrity Gates) Bill

**Status**: In development

**Purpose**: Require integrity certification for AI systems used in public sector.

**Key Provisions**:

1. **Integrity Certification**
   - AI systems above threshold must pass integrity gates
   - Certification by approved sentinel networks
   - Annual recertification required

2. **Transparency Requirements**
   - Decision logging mandatory
   - Explainability standards defined
   - Public reporting of compliance

3. **Enforcement**
   - DSIT enforcement authority
   - Penalties for non-compliance
   - Appeal mechanisms

**Committee**: DSIT (Department for Science, Innovation & Technology)

---

### Digital Governance Framework Bill

**Status**: Concept stage

**Purpose**: Establish comprehensive framework for AI in government.

**Key Provisions**:

1. **National Integrity Infrastructure**
   - Sentinel network deployment
   - Inter-departmental coordination
   - International compatibility

2. **Citizen Rights**
   - Right to explanation
   - Right to appeal
   - Right to opt-out

3. **Democratic Oversight**
   - Parliamentary committee
   - Public consultation requirements
   - Periodic review mandates

## Regulatory Guidance

### FCA Guidance (Proposed)

**Subject**: Classification of MIC tokens

**Recommendation**: Utility token (not e-money, not security)

**Rationale**:
- Earned through work, not investment
- No expectation of profit from others
- Governance utility, not financial return

### ICO Guidance (Proposed)

**Subject**: Data protection in Mobius deployments

**Recommendation**: GDPR-compliant with specified exemptions

**Rationale**:
- Public interest exemption for governance data
- Privacy-by-design for personal data
- Consent-based participation
