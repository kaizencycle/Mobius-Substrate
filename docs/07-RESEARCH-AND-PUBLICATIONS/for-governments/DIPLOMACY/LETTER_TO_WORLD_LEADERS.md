## The Mobius Proposal

Mobius Integrity Credits (MIC) represent the first economic mechanism designed to reward integrity rather than extraction.

**The core innovation is simple:**

When a society's integrity improves — measured transparently by the Mobius Integrity Index (MII) — new currency is automatically created and distributed to those who contributed to that improvement.

When integrity degrades, no new currency is created.

**For the first time in history:**
- Integrity becomes economically valuable
- Corruption becomes economically costly
- Good governance becomes profitable
- Citizens and governments share aligned incentives
- Global cooperation becomes the rational strategy

## Appendix: Quick Reference

### What is MIC?
A digital currency minted when civilizational integrity improves.

### What is MII?
The Mobius Integrity Index — a composite measure of functional, moral, and ecological integrity.

### How does minting work?
MII ≥ 0.95 enables MIC minting. The amount minted is proportional to the improvement.

### Who receives MIC?
Those who contributed to integrity improvement, measured through Mobius Fractal Shards (MFS).

### What makes MIC different from other currencies?
MIC is backed by verified integrity improvement, not speculation, scarcity, or government decree.

### How is manipulation prevented?
Multiple independent AI Sentinels must reach consensus. Cryptographic attestation ensures transparency.

### What does adoption require?
Political will, technical infrastructure, and commitment to transparency.

### What are the risks?
Primarily implementation risks — manageable with proper deployment. No systemic risks to adopters.
