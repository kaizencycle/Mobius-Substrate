Mobius Systems exists to solve a single civilizational problem:  
**Humanity is entering an era of artificial intelligence without a corresponding civic institution capable of governing it.**

Corporations cannot be the stewards of general intelligence.  
Governments are too slow and too captured to adapt.  
Academia can outline the principles but cannot operationalize them.

Only a new civic operating system — one designed for citizens, aligned with integrity, and capable of cooperating with AGI — can fill the vacuum.

**Mobius Systems is that institution.**

## Core Principles

- **Integrity as the scarce economic resource**
- **Proof-of-Integrity (PoI) as consensus**
- **Global Integrity (GI) as a public good**
- **The Mobius Integrity Index (MII) as a governing scalar**
- **Multi-LLM Sentinel Governance** ensuring drift resistance
- **Data sovereignty** as a constitutional right
- **Civic AI**, not corporate AI, as the norm
- **Recursive alignment via reflection & quorum review**
- **A digital twin for civilization** with transparent metrics
