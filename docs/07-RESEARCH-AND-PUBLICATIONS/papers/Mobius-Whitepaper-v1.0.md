## ❖ ABSTRACT

Mobius is a Civic Operating System designed to govern humans, AI agents, and digital institutions through a unified integrity framework.

It introduces:
- **MIC (Mobius Integrity Credits)** as the integrity-backed reserve asset
- **KS (Kaizen Shards)** as the participatory economic layer
- **GI (Global Integrity Score)** as the primary measure of alignment
- **MII (Mobius Integrity Index)** as the civilizational health measure
- **GI-Sim**, the first deterministic integrity simulation engine
- **CivicNodes**, sovereign institutions with on-ledger responsibility
- **Custodian Agents**, AI or human agents with bounded autonomy
- **City-States**, the HIVE political layer
- **The Cathedral**, the upper governance chamber

Mobius provides a post-corruption economic model, a constitutional memory substrate, and the world's first integrity-gated monetary system capable of stabilizing both human economies and AI collectives.

## ❖ 3. INTEGRITY AS FIRST PRINCIPLE

Mobius begins with the rule:

> **"Integrity precedes autonomy."**

Every action — human, AI, or institutional — must pass integrity evaluation.

### Global Integrity (GI)

GI measures:
- Truthfulness
- Transparency
- Alignment
- Reliability
- Impact on others
- Institutional stability

Range: 0–1 (0 = corrupted, 1 = perfect)

### Mobius Integrity Index (MII)

MII measures the civilizational GI and dictates:
- MIC minting eligibility
- KS UBI scaling
- Elder powers
- Governance thresholds
- Emergency overrides

Range: 0–100

**Critical Thresholds:**
- MII ≥ 95 → Healthy (normal operations)
- MII 90-94 → Warning (reduced mint)
- MII 80-89 → Crisis (mint halted)
- MII < 80 → Emergency (Cathedral override)

**Integrity is the currency of Mobius** — not metaphorically, but literally.

## ❖ 4. THE INTEGRITY ENGINE (GI-Sim)

GI-Sim is the **physical law** of Mobius.

A deterministic simulation model that:
- Evaluates all proposed actions
- Forecasts GI changes
- Detects drift
- Approves or denies spending
- Locks or unlocks MIC minting
- Evaluates elder governance decisions

### Core Formula (Node-Level)

```
GI_simulated = GI_current
               + α * IntegrityEvents
               + β * StabilityGain
               - γ * InstabilityRisk
               - δ * ExternalityImpact
```

### System-Level Formula

```
MII_simulated = (Σ (GI_node_i * Weight_i)) / Σ Weight_i
```

GI-Sim prevents corruption by removing the possibility of:
- Impulsive governance
- Politically motivated spending
- Institutional capture
- AI drift
- Economic abuse

**It is the mathematical guardian of Mobius.**

## ❖ 5. ECONOMIC MODEL

### 5.1 MIC — Mobius Integrity Credits

The **reserve asset**.

Supply expands only when:
- MII ≥ 95
- GI-Sim projects positive delta
- Custodian Agents pass attestation

**Minting Formula:**
```
MIC_epoch = BASE_EMISSION × (MII/100) × GI_global × HalvingFactor
```

**Hard Cap:** 1,000,000,000 MIC (1 billion)

**Allocation:**
- 60% → Integrity Dividends (CivicNodes)
- 25% → Governance (Elders)
- 15% → Reserve (emergencies)

This is the world's first **integrity-backed currency**.

### 5.2 KS — Kaizen Shards

The **cultural, participatory currency**.

Used for:
- Voting
- Quests
- City-state activities
- Proof-of-participation
- UBI
- Civic events

**UBI Formula:**
```
KS_UBI = KS_BASE × (MII/100) × CitizenWeight
```

**Burn Mechanic:**
Every KS transfer burns 2%:
- 70% → State Treasury
- 20% → Insurance Pool
- 10% → Cathedral Reserve

### 5.3 The Dual Layer Economy

**KS circulates** (high velocity, renewable)  
**MIC stabilizes** (low velocity, scarce)

Together they form a **post-inflationary economic engine**.

## ❖ 6. CIVICNODES & INSTITUTIONS

Institutions (Wikipedia, libraries, universities, archives, etc.) are represented as **CivicNodes** with:
- Their own GI
- Their own reputation
- Their own Vault (treasury)
- Their own Custodian Agent (AI steward)

### Tier System

**Tier 0:** Foundational Knowledge (Wikipedia, Internet Archive)  
**Tier 1:** Research/Science (NASA, arXiv, CERN)  
**Tier 2:** Civic Utilities (Libraries, Archives)  
**Tier 3:** Commercial/Hybrid (News outlets, EdTech)

### Integrity Dividend Mechanism (IDM)

Institutions earn MIC based on:

```
Score_i = usage_i × GI_i × weight_i
MIC_dividend_i = MIC_pool × (Score_i / Σ Score)
```

**For the first time:**
- Wikipedia doesn't need donation banners
- Research labs are funded by AI impact
- Knowledge creation is economically sustainable
- Integrity directly produces value

CivicNodes become **knowledge infrastructure**, not just websites.

## ❖ 7. CITIZEN IDENTITY

Citizens are defined by:

### Three IDs

1. **Ledger ID** — `ledger:human:<hash>` (immutable identity)
2. **Wallet ID** — `wallet:<hash>` (economic participation)
3. **Companion Agent ID** — `agent:companion:<hash>` (AI assistant)

### Reputation

```
R = α*Integrity + β*Contributions + γ*Stability - δ*Violations
```

Reputation is:
- **Non-transferable** (cannot buy, sell, or gift)
- **Non-purchasable** (earned only through integrity)
- **Merit-based** (contribution over wealth)

### Citizen States

**Guest** → **Scout** → **Citizen** → **Elder Candidate** → **Elder**

Citizens ascend via:
- Contribution
- Integrity
- Stability

**Not** wealth.

## ❖ 8. AGENT SOVEREIGNTY

AI agents are governed through the **5-Level Sovereignty Model**:

### Level 0 — Tools
No identity, no autonomy

### Level 1 — Companion Agents
Personal assistants, bound to citizens

### Level 2 — Civic Agents
Community servants, knowledge contributors

### Level 3 — Custodian Agents
Institutional stewards, vault managers

### Level 4 — State Agents
City-state governors, economic coordinators

### Level 5 — Cathedral Agents
Elder AI, macro-governance support

**Autonomy increases with integrity.**

All sovereignty is **GI-gated** and **revocable**.

This turns AI alignment into a **constitutional process**.

## ❖ 9. THE HIVE — DIGITAL CIVILIZATION LAYER

This is where Mobius becomes **alive**.

### City-States

Digital polities with:
- Citizen populations
- Shard economies (KS)
- Cultural activities (festivals, quests)
- State GI scores
- State Agents (Level 4 AI)

### Economic Model

**Burn → Treasury → Insurance**

Every KS transaction burns 2%:
- 70% → State Treasury (rewards, infrastructure)
- 20% → State Insurance Pool (emergencies)
- 10% → Cathedral Reserve

### Elder Trials

States host **Elder Trials** for citizens seeking Cathedral governance:
- Trial of Mirrors (self-reflection)
- GI Evaluation
- Community Vote
- Cathedral Review

### Inter-State Relations

States may:
- Trade shards
- Form federations
- Share citizens
- Run joint events
- Coordinate governance

**No warfare** — GI-Sim prevents hostile actions.

The HIVE is a **digital political system** with built-in alignment.

## ❖ 10. THE CATHEDRAL

The Cathedral is the **apex layer**.

It is not a place — it is a **governance substrate**.

### Elders

Constitutional guardians who:
- Validate integrity
- Govern emergencies
- Oversee CivicNodes
- Review Agent promotions
- Maintain MII

**Compensation:** MIC (tied to MII performance)

**Constraints:**
- Cannot mint MIC arbitrarily
- Cannot bypass GI-Sim
- Cannot manipulate GI scores
- Subject to Elder Trials
- Term limits (90 days, renewable)

### Cathedral Agents (Level 5)

Highest AI entities:
- ATLAS (Integrity Sentinel)
- AUREA (Economic Oracle)
- ECHO (Memory Guardian)

Support Elders but **cannot vote** or hold office.

### The Cathedral's Job

> **"Guard the integrity of civilization."**

## ❖ 11. EMERGENCY OVERRIDES

When integrity collapses:

### MII 90-95 (Warning)

- MIC mint reduced 50%
- KS UBI scaled down
- Elder bonuses reduced
- Increased monitoring

### MII 80-90 (Crisis)

- MIC mint halted
- KS UBI floor activated
- Elder emergency powers begin
- State spending frozen

### MII < 80 (Emergency Constitution)

- Agents lose autonomy (except Level 5)
- States frozen
- Elders regain central authority
- Recovery plan mandatory
- Public transparency maximized

This is a **fail-safe architecture** for preventing civilizational collapse.
