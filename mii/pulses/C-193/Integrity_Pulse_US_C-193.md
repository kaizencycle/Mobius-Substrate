## ATLAS â€” Structural Topology

From an ATLAS perspective, the U.S. integrity problem is **topological**, not ideological.

> Power has scaled faster than accountability infrastructure.

Three layers are out of sync:

1. **Decision velocity** (fast)
2. **Harm manifestation** (immediate)
3. **Institutional correction** (slow)

When these drift apart, trust decays even if laws remain unchanged.
